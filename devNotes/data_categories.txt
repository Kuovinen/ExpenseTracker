Date – When the expense occurred.

Category – The type of expense (e.g., Food, Transport, Entertainment).

Amount – The cost of the expense.

Description – Additional notes, such as "Dinner at Joe's Diner."

Recurring – Boolean (Yes/No) indicating if the expense is part of a subscription or regular bill.

Tags – User-defined keywords (e.g., "Urgent", "Gift", "Tax Deductible").

Discount – Records any discount applied.
